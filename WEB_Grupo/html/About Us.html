We're an independent studio which developes multiplatform games.

We aspire to be the most player-focused game company in the world. We were established in 2006 by a couple of entrepreneurial gamers who believe player-focused game development can result in awesome games. In 2009, we released our debut title, Monkey Machines, to critical and player acclaim. Over 100 million players every month. We know players form the foundation of our community and it’s for them that we continue to evolve and improve the experience of every game we make.

Since then, we've sold about 2 million copies of our best games.

Among our featured games we can also find Monkey Warriors (2008) and Monkey Race (2013)
